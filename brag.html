Case Studies

A closer look at system restoration, environment hardening, and cloud security work.

01 / ₹50L+ Downtime Losses Prevented

Contained multiple crypto-mining infections across production servers without service interruption. Isolated dormant scripts and the cron jobs that reactivated the malware, developed cleanup automation, and restored affected systems under live traffic. Applied OWASP and CIS hardening, introduced firewall controls, and blocked recurring IP ranges. Result: approximately ₹50L+ in potential downtime losses avoided, and post-incident audits validated continued system stability.

02 / 40-60% Regression Reduction

Standardized production, staging, and test environments to eliminate inconsistent deployments. Implemented role-based access controls, patch management, and a clear separation of duties. Integrated ELK observability for early defect detection and proactive monitoring. Result: regression frequency reduced by 40-60% within a quarter, and the environment passed cyber-insurance compliance review.

03 / 10+ Years of Cloud & Security Leadership

Directed AWS infrastructure and security operations through several organizational transitions at Neviton. Implemented a Wazuh + TheHive SIEM integrated with QuickSight analytics to improve incident visibility. Aligned systems with ISO 27001:2022 and CFR Part 11, coordinating response efforts under HIPAA and K-12 data governance constraints. Result: sustained audit readiness, zero critical outages, and consistent compliance performance across a decade.
